GAME@TRIX
Eat. Sleep. Game. Repeat
  1. The event is open for classes IX-XII.
  2. Only 1 team comprising of 3 students can participate from each school.
  3. This event will be held in two phases - an online prelims round followed by the final round on the day of Ordin@trix 2020.
  4. Only 16 teams will qualify for the final round.
  5. The prelims will be held in July 2020.
  6. The games will be played on the following platforms - PC AND CONSOLE .
  7. Students are allowed to bring their own devices such as controllers, keyboards and mouse. Thumb grips are allowed, pro controllers/scuff controllers are not allowed.
  8. Specific Team requirements- Out of 3, 1 team member must be a console player and 1 must be a PC player, 3rd member can of either platform.
  9. The event includes team rounds- they may include games that are cross platform and games specific to a platform as well. So choose team members accordingly.
  10. Details of a discord server will be shared upon registrations. It would include details about the prelims and final event.
