QUIZ@TRIX
Time to put your knowledge to test, little quizzer
  1. The event is open for students of classes IX-XII.
  2. Only 1 team comprising of 2 students can participate from each school.
  3. This event will be conducted in two phases: an Online Prelims (Crossword) followed by an Online Final.
  4. Only the top 6 teams will qualify for on the spot Finals. The list of selected teams will be uploaded latest by 20th July 2020.
  5. Questions can be asked from any aspect of the world of technology and related industries.
  6. The quiz would consist of 4 rounds:
    1. General Round- This round would consist of 12 questions and each team would be given 20 seconds to answer them.
      20 points for every correct answer and 10 points for every correct answer if the question has been passed. There will be no negative marking.
      If a team fails to answer a question within 20 seconds then the question automatically gets passed to the next team.
    2. Audio-Visual Round- This round would have an image or an audio followed by a question.
      In this round there would be 6 questions.
      15 points for every correct answer and -5 for every wrong one.
      There would not be any passing of questions in this round.
      Each team would be given 30 seconds to answer.
    3. Rapid fire- 30 questions in all.
      Each team would be given 5 seconds to answer a question.
      10 points for every correct answer and -5 for every wrong one.
      There would be no passing in this round.
    4. Connect the dots- There would be several pictures displayed on the screen and the participants would have to guess the possible answer after relating the images to one another.
      There would be 6 questions in all and 10 points for every correct answer and no negative marking.
      Passing is allowed and the team which gets the correct answer after passing would be given 5 points.
      Each team would be given 30 seconds to answer.
  7. A surprise round would be conducted in case of a tie.
